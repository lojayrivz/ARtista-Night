# Summary

I turned the Raspberry Pi logo into a 3D model!

You can scale the model up or down however you'd like within the slicer software; it will fit on pretty small printers as is; however, if you scale it down too much some of the details may not come through.

If you print the model, then feel free to upload a picture of it under the 'I Made One' section!
